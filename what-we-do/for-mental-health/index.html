For Mental Health

Creative Wellbeing Workshops for adults living in Bath and North East Somerset

Interested in trying out a new art form and developing skills in a relaxed atmosphere?

Creativity Works’ Mental Health Creative Support Service offers an opportunity to learn some creative skills and make new friends. The service includes workshops led by experienced artists and creative peer support groups managed by group members and occasional visiting art tutors.

The workshops and groups are for people who are in the process of recovery from ongoing mental health challenges, including anxiety and depression. They help to build confidence, learn new skills and make friends.

Please see our latest workshops and groups below, and for further information please get in touch with Philippa: Email philippa@creativityworks.org.uk or call 07715 382877.
